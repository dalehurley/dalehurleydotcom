# Microsoft for Startups - Empowering the Next Generation of Innovators

A friend of mine recently showed me an awesome programme that Microsoft has for startups. It's called Microsoft for Startups, and it's designed to help entrepreneurs and innovators bring their ideas to life.

Microsoft for Startups provides access to technology, business, and marketing resources that can help startups grow and scale. The programme offers a range of benefits, including free Azure credits, technical support, and co-selling opportunities with Microsoft's global sales team.

<blockquote>
    Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub helps startups radically accelerate
    innovation by providing access to industry-leading AI services, expert
    guidance, and the essential technology needed to build a future-proofed
    startup. Open to all—sign up in minutes with no funding required.
</blockquote>

The best part is that Microsoft for Startups is open to startups of all sizes and stages. Whether you're just starting out or looking to scale your business, Microsoft for Startups can help you achieve your goals.

The URL for the Microsoft for Startups programme is [https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/startups](https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/startups).
