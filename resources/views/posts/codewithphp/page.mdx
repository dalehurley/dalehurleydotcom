## Welcome to CodeWithPHP.com — Your Free Guide to Learning PHP

Hi, I’m Dale Hurley—AI-driven tech entrepreneur, banking innovation leader, and lifelong PHP fan. I built [CodeWithPHP.com](https://codewithphp.com) to give aspiring developers a practical, modern, and completely free starting point for backend development. PHP still powers a huge slice of the internet, yet the best guidance is either outdated or locked away. This platform is my answer: clear explanations, real examples, and zero paywalls.

## Why I Created CodeWithPHP.com

- **Make high-quality PHP learning free:** Too many platforms hide the basics behind subscriptions. CodeWithPHP.com puts the fundamentals within reach for anyone with curiosity and Wi-Fi.
- **Provide modern, beginner-friendly tutorials:** PHP has evolved, so every lesson leans on current language features, readable syntax, and approachable context.
- **Help new developers build real skills:** Each guide favors clarity, repetition, and hands-on exercises, so you gain confidence while you learn.

## What You’ll Find on the Platform

- **PHP basics for beginners:** Variables, functions, loops, arrays, forms, sessions—everything explained in plain language.
- **Real coding examples:** Copy, run, tweak, and break simple scripts so concepts stick.
- **Beginner-friendly projects:** Build small apps that connect the dots between syntax and shipping.
- **Regular free updates:** Fresh tutorials roll out weekly so you always have something new to tackle.
- **Zero ads, zero paywalls:** Just focused learning, no distractions, no upsells.

## Who CodeWithPHP.com Is For

- Complete beginners writing PHP for the first time
- Self-taught developers craving clean explanations
- Students learning backend concepts at school or bootcamps
- Hobbyists building personal projects or MVPs
- Anyone curious about how PHP works under the hood

If you want to learn quickly without frustration, you’re in the right place.

## How to Get Started

1. Visit [CodeWithPHP.com](https://codewithphp.com).
2. Start with the PHP Basics track.
3. Type every example, experiment, and break it on purpose.
4. Bookmark the site so you can catch each new tutorial as it drops.

Learning sticks best when you build alongside each lesson, so open your editor and follow along in real time.

## Why CodeWithPHP.com Is Completely Free

I believe access shouldn’t be the barrier to a tech career. Keeping the platform 100% free—with no hidden tiers, ads, or sponsorship noise—means more people can build skills, land internships, or ship their first product without debt. If it helps you, sharing it with a friend or classmate is the best way to pay it forward.
